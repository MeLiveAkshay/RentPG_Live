About RentPG Live

RentPG Live is a student- and professional-focused platform helping people discover safe and verified PG accommodations with ease. We aim to bring trust and simplicity to the rental experience.

Our Mission

To make rental search stress-free through verified listings, transparent details, and real-time availability.

Our Vision

To become the most reliable PG rental platform for students and working individuals across India.

Our Values

Trust, Transparency, User-first Experience, and Innovation in digital renting.
